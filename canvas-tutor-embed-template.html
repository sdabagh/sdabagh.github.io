How the AI Tutor Works

This AI tutor provides adaptive support that adjusts to your needs:

  • Level 1 (Hints): Minimal guidance - helps you think independently
  • Level 2 (Guided): Step-by-step questions to walk through problems
  • Level 3 (Examples): Worked examples with detailed explanations

The tutor automatically adapts based on your progress. No need to select a level manually!

Quick Tips for Best Results

  • Try the problem yourself first before asking for help
  • Be specific about what you're struggling with
  • Ask follow-up questions if something isn't clear
  • Use the "Hint" button if you just need a small nudge
