Study Tips

How to Use Videos Effectively

  • Watch BEFORE class: Preview topics to prepare
  • Take notes: Pause and write down key points
  • Practice along: Work through examples with the video
  • Don't just watch: Do practice problems after watching

Study Schedule Recommendations

  • Daily practice: 30-60 minutes every day beats cramming
  • Use multiple resources: Textbook, videos, tutoring, office hours
  • Start homework early: Give yourself time to get help if stuck
  • Form study groups: Explaining to others helps you learn
