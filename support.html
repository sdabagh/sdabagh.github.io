Workshops & Tutoring

Book Safaa for private tutoring, group workshops, or speaking engagements. All sessions are available in English or Arabic.

  • 📊

    Statistics Tutoring

    One-on-one or small group tutoring for introductory statistics, probability, and data analysis. College level and above.

  • 💰

    Financial Literacy Workshop

    Interactive workshop covering budgeting, saving, investing basics, and the 70/20/10 rule. For individuals, schools, or community groups.

  • 💻

    Python & Data Science Tutoring

    Learn Python, pandas, data visualization, and machine learning basics. Hands-on projects with real data.

  • 🧠

    AI in Education Workshop

    For teachers and institutions: how to integrate AI tools into your classroom effectively and ethically.

  • 📈

    Gold & Market Analysis

    Understanding gold prices, market trends, and building predictive models. For data enthusiasts and investors.

    Keynotes and panel talks on educational equity, AI in education, financial literacy, and the immigrant educator experience.
