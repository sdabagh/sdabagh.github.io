Welcome

I'm Safaa Dabagh -- a community college mathematics instructor, UCLA Statistics PhD candidate, and advocate for educational equity through technology.

Bridging research and practice to make statistics education accessible, effective, and empowering for all learners.

My Story

I'm a Lebanese-American mom of five who started at community college, transferred to UCLA, and earned degrees in Mathematics/Economics and Statistics. My journey wasn't linear -- I paused my PhD when I lost my mother. But now, more than ever, I'm committed to completing my dissertation and using my research to help students like those I teach every day.

My PhD research investigates how AI-powered cognitive scaffolding systems can improve learning outcomes in introductory statistics, with particular attention to diverse student populations across different institutional contexts.
