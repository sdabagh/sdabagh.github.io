My Story

I'm a Lebanese-American mom of five who came to the U.S. determined to build a new life. I started at community college, transferred to UCLA, and earned degrees in Math/Economics and Statistics. Now I teach across multiple institutions and am completing my PhD dissertation on AI-powered education.

My children — Adam, Aya, Abed, Issa, and Zachariah — are my greatest inspiration.
