Learning Objectives

By the end of this session, you will be able to:

  • Use unit pricing to compare products and find the best deal
  • Distinguish between price and value in purchasing decisions
  • Identify impulse buying triggers and develop strategies to resist them
  • Understand halal consumption and the concept of Shukr (gratitude)

Why This Matters

Smart spending is not about being cheap — it is about getting the most value from every dollar. This session teaches you to be intentional with purchases.
