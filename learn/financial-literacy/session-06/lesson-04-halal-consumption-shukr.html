Check Your Understanding

1. What does halal consumption mean beyond food?

Answer: Halal consumption means ensuring that what you buy, how you earn money, and how you spend are all permissible and ethical. It applies to all purchases, not just food.

2. What is Shukr?

Answer: Shukr means gratitude in Arabic. In the context of spending, it means appreciating what you have rather than always wanting more. Grateful people tend to make wiser spending choices.

3. How does gratitude affect spending habits?

Answer: When you are grateful for what you have, you feel less pressure to buy more. Gratitude reduces impulse buying and increases contentment with what you already own.

4. What does mindful consumption look like in practice?

Answer: Buying with intention (not impulse), choosing quality over quantity, avoiding waste, being thankful for purchases, and considering the ethical impact of what you buy.
