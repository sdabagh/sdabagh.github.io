Test What You Learned!

8 multiple-choice questions about Session 6: Smart Spending. Get 6 or more right to pass!

How it works:

  • 8 multiple-choice questions
  • Select one answer per question
  • Click "Submit Quiz" when done
  • Need 6/8 to pass
  • Retake as many times as you want

Progress

1

What is unit pricing?

  • The total price of one item
  • The cost per unit (per ounce, per serving, etc.)
  • The sale price of a product
  • The price after tax
2

Which is the better deal: $4.50 for 15 oz or $3.00 for 8 oz?

  • $3.00 for 8 oz because it costs less
  • $4.50 for 15 oz because the unit price is lower ($0.30/oz vs $0.375/oz)
  • They are the same value
  • You cannot compare them
3

What is the difference between price and value?

  • They mean the same thing
  • Price is what you pay; value is what you get for your money
  • Price is for expensive items; value is for cheap items
  • Value is always higher than price
4

What is the 24-hour rule?

  • Return items within 24 hours
  • Wait 24 hours before making an unplanned purchase
  • Shop for only 24 hours per month
  • Spend no more than $24 per day
5

What is impulse buying?

  • Carefully planned shopping
  • Unplanned purchases driven by emotion
  • Buying only what you need
  • Shopping with a list
6

What does Shukr mean?

  • Spending wisely
  • Gratitude
  • Saving money
  • Budgeting
7

How does gratitude affect spending?

  • It makes you spend more
  • It makes you feel less pressure to buy more
  • It has no effect on spending
  • It makes you feel guilty about buying things
8

What does halal consumption mean beyond food?

  • It only applies to food
  • Ensuring all earning and spending is permissible and ethical
  • Buying the most expensive version
  • Shopping at specific stores
