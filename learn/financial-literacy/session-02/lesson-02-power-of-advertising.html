Check Your Understanding

1. Name two tricks advertisers use to make you want something.

Answer: Common tricks include: creating urgency (limited time!), using celebrities, creating FOMO (fear of missing out), and emotional appeals.

2. What does FOMO stand for and how do advertisers use it?

Answer: Fear Of Missing Out. Advertisers show everyone else having fun with a product to make you feel left out if you do not buy it.

3. How can you think critically about an ad?

Answer: Ask: What are they trying to sell? What trick are they using? Do I actually need this, or does the ad just make me want it? Who benefits if I buy?

4. Give an example of an ad making a want feel like a need.

Answer: A phone company ad showing everyone with the newest model makes the old phone feel inadequate, turning an upgrade from a want into a felt need.
