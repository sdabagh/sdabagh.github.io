Session 2 Family Guide

Money, Values & You — Tips for families facilitating Session 2: Needs vs. Wants

Overview

Learn to tell the difference between what you need and what you want, how advertising blurs the line, and the Islamic principles of moderation.

Facilitating Each Lesson

Lesson 1: What Do You Really Need?

Tip: Explore the difference between survival needs and everything else. Discover why the line between needs and wants is not always clear. Take time to discuss each topic and let your child share their thoughts before providing answers.
Try asking: "What do you think about what are needs? (food, shelter, water, clothing, healthcare)?"

Lesson 2: The Power of Advertising

Tip: Discover how ads shape what we think we need. Learn to spot advertising tricks like urgency, celebrity endorsement, and FOMO. Take time to discuss each topic and let your child share their thoughts before providing answers.
Try asking: "What do you think about ads are everywhere: tv, social media, billboards, even schools?"

Lesson 3: Opportunity Cost

Tip: Every choice has a cost. Learn what you give up every time you spend money, and how to make better decisions. Take time to discuss each topic and let your child share their thoughts before providing answers.
Try asking: "What do you think about what is opportunity cost: what you give up when you choose?"

Lesson 4: Israf and Qasad: Balance in Spending

Tip: Explore the Islamic concepts of Israf (wastefulness) and Qasad (moderation). Learn what Quran 7:31 teaches about balance. Take time to discuss each topic and let your child share their thoughts before providing answers.
Try asking: "What do you think about what is israf (wastefulness) in islam?"

Conversation Starters for Home

  • "What did you learn today about what are needs? (food, shelter, water, clothing, healthcare)?"
  • "What did you learn today about ads are everywhere: tv, social media, billboards, even schools?"
  • "What did you learn today about what is opportunity cost: what you give up when you choose?"
  • "What did you learn today about what is israf (wastefulness) in islam?"
