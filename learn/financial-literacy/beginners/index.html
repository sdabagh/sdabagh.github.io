Financial Literacy for Beginners

If your income stops tomorrow, how long can you survive?

This course will help you answer that question — and fix it.

Start from zero. Learn at your own pace. No tests, no pressure.

12 Modules English + Arabic ND-Friendly Free
Module 1

What Is Money?

The history of money, different types, and how it flows through the economy.

 Module 2

Income: Where Money Comes From

Salary, freelance work, side income, and the difference between gross and net pay.

 Module 3

Expenses: Where Money Goes

Needs vs. wants, fixed vs. variable costs, and tracking where your money goes.

 Module 4

The 70/20/10 Rule

A simple budget blueprint that works for any income level.

 Module 5

Saving: Building Your Safety Net

Emergency funds, the pay-yourself-first method, and making saving automatic.

 Module 6

Debt: Understanding Borrowing

Good debt vs. bad debt, how interest works, and strategies to pay it off.

 Module 7

Credit Scores: Your Financial Reputation

What credit scores are, why they matter, and how to build yours.

 Module 8

Investing Basics: Making Money Work for You

Compound interest, stocks, ETFs, and the path to building wealth.

 Module 9

Banking: Choosing and Using Accounts

Checking, savings, online banking, and avoiding fees.

 Module 10

Insurance and Protection

Health, car, renter's, and life insurance fundamentals.

 Module 11

Taxes: What You Need to Know

How income tax works, tax brackets, filing, and deductions.

 Module 12

Planning Your Financial Future

Goals, retirement, multiple income streams, and your action plan.
