Check Your Understanding

1. What is risk in investing?

Answer: Risk is the chance that you could lose some or all of the money you invested. Higher-risk investments can lose value quickly.

2. What is the relationship between risk and reward?

Answer: Generally, higher potential rewards come with higher risks. Safe investments grow slowly; risky investments might grow fast but could also lose value.

3. What is diversification?

Answer: Diversification means spreading your investments across different types so that if one loses value, others might still do well. It is the 'do not put all your eggs in one basket' principle.

4. Is it possible to invest with zero risk?

Answer: No investment is completely risk-free. Even savings accounts have the risk that inflation will outpace your interest earnings. The key is managing risk, not eliminating it.

Key Takeaways

  • What is risk: the chance of losing some or all of your money
  • What is reward: the potential gain from an investment
