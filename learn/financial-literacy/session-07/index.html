Learn what investing means, the basics of risk and reward, and how halal investing screens for values.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this session, you will be able to:

  • Explain what investing means: ownership vs. lending
  • Describe the relationship between risk and reward
  • Understand what stocks and businesses are at a basic level
  • Explain halal investing, industry screening, and the concept of sukuk

Why This Matters

Investing is how money grows over time. Understanding the basics now gives you a head start on building wealth responsibly.
