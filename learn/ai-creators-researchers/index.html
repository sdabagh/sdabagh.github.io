A course for people who take AI seriously

If you have taken the free AI Certificate in 28 Days, this is the natural next step. Each day here goes deeper: you will not just try a tool, you will set it up properly, use it on real work, and learn when not to reach for it. Content is designed for creators, researchers, graduate students, and knowledge-work professionals who want AI to compound their expertise rather than replace it.

01

Depth, not just breadth

Every day is ~30 minutes, with setup notes, a full walkthrough, and two exercises (beginner + advanced) you can do on real work.

02

Honest comparisons

Each tool is placed in context against its alternatives, with clear guidance on when to use it and when another tool is better.

03

A real capstone

Day 29 asks you to chain three tools into one real project you actually need to ship — not a toy prompt. Day 30 issues your certificate.

04

Privacy-first progress

Your progress is saved locally in your browser. No account, no tracking, no email list. The certificate is generated in your browser and downloaded as a PDF.

The 30-day curriculum

Six themed weeks plus a capstone. Click any day to jump in.

Week 1
Deep Research
Tools that read, search, and synthesize the scholarly web
Day 01 NotebookLM Upload your sources. Ask anything. Get cited answers. Day 02 Perplexity Deep Research An AI that takes ten minutes to actually research your question. Day 03 Elicit Literature review across thousands of academic papers. Day 04 Consensus Does the evidence say yes or no? Day 05 Claude Projects A persistent workspace where Claude keeps your context for months.
Week 2
Coding & Vibe-Building
AI-native development environments and no-code builders
Day 06 Cursor The IDE that thinks alongside you. Day 07 Windsurf An agentic IDE that can take multi-step actions on its own. Day 08 GitHub Copilot The AI that finishes your sentence — line by line, inside your editor. Day 09 v0 by Vercel Describe a UI in English; get production-quality React components. Day 10 Bolt.new Describe an app. Get a full-stack deployed app.
Week 3
Voice & Audio
Speech synthesis, transcription, editing, and music generation
Day 11 ElevenLabs Human-quality AI voice for narration, cloning, and dubbing. Day 12 Otter.ai Meeting transcription, summaries, and action items — automatically. Day 13 Descript Edit audio and video by editing the transcript. Day 14 Suno Generate a real, full-length song from a text prompt. Day 15 Adobe Podcast The magic button that makes any recording sound studio-quality.
Week 4
Video Creation
Generative video, avatar presenters, text-to-video, and social clips

Day 18 Pictory Turn a blog post into a narrated video with stock footage — automatically. Day 19 Opus Clip Turn a long video into a dozen viral-ready shorts in minutes. Day 20 Captions The AI video-editor app that lives on your phone.
Week 5
Business Power Tools
Presentations, lead enrichment, automation, and professional writing

Week 6
College Student Toolkit

Day 25 MathGPT A math solver that shows its work, not just the answer. Day 26 Sizzle AI A Socratic tutor that walks you through STEM problems at your pace. Day 27 QuillBot The writing workshop that runs inside your essay. Day 28 SciSpace An AI that reads academic papers with you, one paragraph at a time.
Week 7
Capstone & Certificate
A real project using multiple tools, plus your certificate
Day 29 Capstone Project One real project. At least three tools from this course. Chained into a workflow. Day 30 Certificate & Reflection Claim your certificate. Decide what comes next.

How to use this course

  • 30 minutes a day. Each day is designed to fit in one focused sitting.
  • Do the advanced exercise, not just the walkthrough. Reading is necessary; doing is where learning sticks.
  • Mark days complete as you finish. Your progress syncs to the Day 30 certificate gate.
  • Skip around if you want. Most days stand alone. The capstone is the only one that assumes the rest.
  • Return for reference. Each day's comparison table is designed to be a bookmark you come back to.
