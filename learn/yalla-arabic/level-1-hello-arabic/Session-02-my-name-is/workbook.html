Print this page. Use it during the session. Keep it in your folder.

🟢 1. Today's letter — ب (ba)

Trace each ba. Start on the right. Make a little smile, then drop one dot underneath.

ب . . . . ب . . . . ب . . . . ب . . . . ب

Now try writing one by yourself:

Big ba! Make it huge — don't forget the dot:

The dot is the most important part. Without the dot, it's a different letter!

🟡 2. Find the ba

Circle every word that has the letter ب (ba) in it:

بَابا     اِسمي     أَهلاً     حَبيبي     أنا

Hint: look for the little dot underneath!

🟠 3. I can read these words

Read these three words out loud. Point to each one as you say it:

Picture Arabic Say it
🙋
اِسمي
 is-mee
👨
بَابا
 baa-baa
👉
أنا
 a-na

Now try this whole sentence:

أنا اِسمي ...
(ana ismi ...) — and add your own name at the end!

🟣 4. Match the question to the answer

Draw a line from each question on the left to the right answer on the right.

🌟 6. Mini-challenge (optional stretch)

Ask THREE people their name in Arabic today. Use

ما اِسمَك؟
for boys and men, and
ما اِسمِك؟
for girls and women.

Person Boy or girl? Their name
1. _____________ b / g _______________
2. _____________ b / g _______________
3. _____________ b / g _______________

Pets count too. So do stuffed animals.

📝 Tonight's home practice (1 minute)

Find someone at home tonight — Baba, Mama, a sibling, a grandparent on the phone. Introduce yourself in Arabic:

**أنا اِسمي ...**

Then ask them their name in Arabic:
