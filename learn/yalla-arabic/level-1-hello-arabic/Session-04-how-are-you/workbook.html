🟢 1. Today's letter — ث (tha)

This letter says "th" like in think. It looks like a little boat with three dots on top! 🛶

Trace each tha. Start with the boat shape, then add three dots above:

ث . . . . ث . . . . ث . . . . ث . . . . ث

Now try writing one by yourself:

Big tha! Don't forget the three dots:

🟡 2. Find the tha

Circle every word that has the letter ث (tha) in it:

ثَلاثة     مْنيح     ثَوب     تَمام     ثَعلَب

Hint: look for the three dots on top!

🟠 3. I can read these words

After you read them, ask a grown-up: "How are YOU today?" Then listen to their answer.

🌟 6. Mini-challenge (optional stretch)

Ask three people in your family "كيفَك؟" (to a boy) or "كيفِك؟" (to a girl). Write down what they said!

Bonus: did anyone say

الحَمدُ لله
(al-hamdu lillah — "all is well, thanks")? Almost every Arab grown-up says this! It's how we answer "how are you" — even when we feel just okay.
