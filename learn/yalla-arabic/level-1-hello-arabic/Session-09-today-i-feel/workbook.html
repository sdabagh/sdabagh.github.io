🟢 1. Letter review — write your favorites

This week is a review week — no new letter today! Pick two letters you've learned so far and write each one three times.

My first letter is: _______

My second letter is: _______

Tip: if you can't remember any, peek at an old workbook. That's allowed!

🟡 2. Find the feeling words

Circle every word below that means a feeling:

مَبسوط     بابا     زَعلان     تُفّاحة     خايِف     تَعبان

Hint: four of them are feelings. Two of them are NOT.

🟠 3. I can read these words

Read these three words out loud. Point to each face as you say it:

🌟 6. Mini-challenge (optional stretch)

In Arabic, feeling words change depending on if you're a boy or a girl. Boys add nothing. Girls add a little ة at the end (it sounds like "eh" or "ah").

Fill in the missing form:

Boy says Girl says
مَبسوط
مَبسوطة
زَعلان
_____________
تَعبان
_____________

Which one do YOU say? Circle it: boy form / girl form
