🟠 3. I can read these words

Read these words out loud. Point to each one as you say it:

When to say it Arabic Say it

After you read them, say each one in a tiny whisper voice. Then in a big proud voice.

🌟 6. Mini-challenge (optional stretch)

In Arabic, please changes depending on who you're talking to:

  • Talking to a boy or man →
    مِن فَضلَك
    (min fadlak)
  • Talking to a girl or woman →
    مِن فَضلِك
    (min fadlik)

Fill in which one you'd say:

Who I'm asking Which "please"?
My baba
_____________
My mama
_____________
My brother
_____________
My teacher (a woman)
_____________

Tiny tip: the difference is one little vowel mark at the end. Listen for the "ak" vs the "ik."
