🟢 1. Today's letter — ت (ta)

Trace each ta. Start with the bowl, then add two dots on top.

ت . . . . ت . . . . ت . . . . ت . . . . ت

Now try writing one by yourself. Don't forget the two dots!

🟡 2. Find the ta

Circle every word that has the letter ت (ta) in it:

تيتا     بابا     بَيت     أُخت     ماما

Hint: three of them have a ta. Look for the two little dots!

🌟 6. Mini-challenge (optional stretch)

Count the people in your family. Write the number, then say this out loud:

**عَيلتي فيها _____ ناس.**

(ʿayilti fiha _____ nas — "My family has _____ people.")

How many people in my family? Do I have an أَخ? Do I have an أُخت?
_______ yes / no yes / no

Some families are big, some are small, some live close, some live far. Every عَيلة counts.
