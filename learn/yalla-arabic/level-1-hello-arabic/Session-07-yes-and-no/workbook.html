🟢 1. Today's letter — خ (kha)

The خ sounds like you're clearing your throat — a soft scratchy sound at the back of your mouth. It has one dot on top.

Trace each kha. Start with the round belly, then add the dot up high.

خ . . . . خ . . . . خ . . . . خ . . . . خ

Now try writing one by yourself:

🟡 2. Find the kha

Circle every word that has the letter خ (kha) in it:

خَلاص     نَعَم     الخَير     لا     أَخ

Hint: look for the dot on top!

🟠 3. I can read these words

Read these three words out loud. Point to each one as you say it:

Meaning Arabic Say it
✅ yes
نَعَم
 na-'am
❌ no
لا
 la
👌 okay / done
خَلاص
 kha-las

After you read them, ask a grown-up to ask you a silly question. Answer with one of these words!

🟣 4. Match the answer to the question

Draw a line from each question on the left to the best answer on the right.

Question Answer
Do you want ice cream? ─ →
أَكيد!
(for sure!)
Is the sky green? ─ →
لا
(no)
Will it rain tomorrow? ─ →
يُمكِن
(maybe)
What's the capital of Lebanon? ─ →
ما بَعرَف
(I don't know)

It's okay to say "I don't know"! That's a real answer too.
