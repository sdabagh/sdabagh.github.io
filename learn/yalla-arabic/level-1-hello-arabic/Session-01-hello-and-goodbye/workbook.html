🟢 1. Today's letter — أ (alif)

Trace each alif. Start at the top. Pull straight down.

أ . . . . أ . . . . أ . . . . أ . . . . أ

Now try writing one by yourself:

🟡 2. Find the alif

Circle every word that has the letter أ (alif) in it:

أَهلاً     مَرحَبا     شُكراً     أنا     يَلّا

Hint: most of them do!

🟠 3. I can read these words

Read these two words out loud. Point to each one as you say it:

Picture Arabic Say it
👋
مَرحَبا
 mar-ha-ba
🤗
أَهلاً
 ah-lan

After you read them, ask a grown-up: "Did I say it right?" Tell them: "Good job for asking."

🟣 4. Match the greeting to the time

Draw a line from each greeting on the left to the right time of day on the right.

Greeting Time

مَساء الخَير
 ─ → 🌅 Morning
مع السَّلامة
 ─ → 👋 Anytime — when leaving

🌟 6. Mini-challenge (optional stretch)

Write your own name. Then, next to it, write the first letter of your name.

My name First sound Is it an alif?
_____________ _______ yes / no

If your name starts with an "ah" sound, your first letter is probably an أ alif too! Some Arabic names that start with alif:

**آدَم** (Adam)    **أَحمَد** (Ahmad)    **أَمَل** (Amal)    **أَمير** (Ameer)    **أَسماء** (Asma)
