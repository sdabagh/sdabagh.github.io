🟢 1. Today's numerals — ٦ ٧ ٨ ٩ ١٠

Trace each Arabic numeral. Say its name out loud as you trace.

٦ . . . . ٦ . . . . ٦ . . . . ٦    (سِتّة — sitteh)

٧ . . . . ٧ . . . . ٧ . . . . ٧    (سَبعة — sab'a)

٨ . . . . ٨ . . . . ٨ . . . . ٨    (ثَمانية — thmaniyeh)

٩ . . . . ٩ . . . . ٩ . . . . ٩    (تِسعة — tis'a)

١٠ . . . ١٠ . . . ١٠ . . . ١٠    ('ashra — عَشرة)

Now try writing each one by yourself:

٦ ٧ ٨ ٩ ١٠

Tip: ٦ looks like a backwards 7. ٧ looks like an upside-down V. ٨ looks like an upside-down V too, but pointier!

🌟 6. Mini-challenge (optional stretch)

Count all the way from 1 to 10 in Arabic without stopping. Then try it backwards from 10 to 1!
