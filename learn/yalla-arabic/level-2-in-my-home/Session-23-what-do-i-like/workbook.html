🟢 1. Today's writing — بِحِبّ

Today we're not learning a new letter — we're practicing a whole word: بِحِبّ (I love / I like).

Trace the word. Go right to left. Feel the little shadda (ّ) — that's the "double squeeze" on the last letter.

بِحِبّ . . . . بِحِبّ . . . . بِحِبّ . . . . بِحِبّ

Now try writing it on your own. Right to left!

Now write the opposite — مَا بِحِبّ (I don't like):

مَا بِحِبّ . . . . مَا بِحِبّ . . . . مَا بِحِبّ

🟡 2. Find the letter ب (baa)

Circle every word that has the letter ب in it. Look carefully — ب has a dot under it.

بِحِبّ     شو     كَتير     حَليب     شَوي     بَندورة

Hint: four of them have a ب somewhere!

🟠 3. I can read these words

Read each word out loud. Point as you say it.
