🟢 1. Today's letter — س (sin)

Trace each sin. Start on the right. Make three little teeth, then a smooth bowl underneath.

س . . . . س . . . . س . . . . س . . . . س

Now try writing one by yourself:

Big sin! Make it huge — really stretch those teeth:

Tip: سsin sounds like the "s" in "salad."

🟡 2. Find the sin

Circle every word that has the letter س (sin) in it:

سَلَطة     بَندورة     بَقدونِس     خِيار     بَصَل

Hint: sin can hide at the start, the middle, or the end of a word. Look carefully!

🟠 3. I can read these words

Read these three words out loud. Point to each one as you say it:

After you read them, ask a grown-up: "Want me to make a salad with you?"

🟣 4. Sort it — salad or not salad?

Tito is making fattoush. Draw a line from each vegetable to the right bowl.
