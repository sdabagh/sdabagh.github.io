Print this page. Use it during the session. Keep it in your folder. Today is a project day — take your time!

🟢 1. Letter warm-up — all the letters you know

This is a review session, so let's warm up the writing hand. Trace these letters from Level 2. Say each one out loud as you trace it:

ب . . . ت . . . ث . . . ج . . . ح . . . خ . . . د . . . ذ . . . ر . . . ز

Now pick your two favorite letters and write them big here:

My favorite letter My second favorite

Whisper to yourself: "I know these letters now." Because you do.

🟡 2. Find the word — بَيتي

Circle every place you see the word بَيتي (my house):

بَيتي كَبير     هَذا بَيت     بَيتي جَميل     في بَيتي مَطبَخ     بَيت صَديقي

Hint: look for the little ي at the end — that's the "my" part.

🟠 3. I can read these words

Read each word out loud. Point as you say it:
