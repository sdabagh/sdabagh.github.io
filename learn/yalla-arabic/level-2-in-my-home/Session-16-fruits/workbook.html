🟢 1. Today's letter — ز (zay)

Trace each zay. Start at the top left. Curve down like a little smile, then add the dot on top.

ز . . . . ز . . . . ز . . . . ز . . . . ز

Now try writing one by yourself. Don't forget the dot!

Big zay! Make it huge (and put a big fat dot on top):

Tip: ز looks a lot like ر (ra), but ز has a dot. One little dot changes the whole letter!

🟡 2. Find the zay

Circle every word that has the letter ز (zay) in it:

زَبيب     مَوز     تُفّاح     عِنَب     رُمَّان     يَلّا

Hint: two of these fruits have a zay hiding in them.

🌟 6. Mini-challenge (optional stretch)

Did you know زَبيب (raisins) are just dried عِنَب (grapes)? Same fruit, two names!

Can you think of other fruits that change names when they dry? Ask a grown-up:

Fresh fruit When it's dried, it's called...
عِنَب (grapes)
زَبيب (raisins)
🫐 figs (fresh) _________________
🍑 _________________ _________________

Bonus: both of these dried-fruit answers start with the letter ز. Sneaky!
