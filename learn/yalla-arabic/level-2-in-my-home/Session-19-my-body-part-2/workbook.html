🟢 1. Today's letter — ص (sad)

Trace each ṣād. Start with the big oval. Then add the tail.

ص . . . . ص . . . . ص . . . . ص . . . . ص

Now try writing one by yourself:

Big ṣād! Make it huge:

Tip: ص is a "heavy" sound. Say it from the back of your mouth — like a strong S.

🟡 2. Find the ṣād

Circle every word that has the letter ص (sad) in it:

صَدر     يَد     صَباح     قَلب     صَديق     بَطن

Hint: there are three!

🌟 6. Mini-challenge (optional stretch)

Your قَلب (heart) lives inside your صَدر (chest). Put your hand on your chest. Can you feel it beating?

Count how many heartbeats you feel in 10 seconds:

My heartbeats in 10 seconds After jumping 20 times
_______ _______

Then say out loud:

**قَلبي قَوي!**

(qalbi qawi — "My heart is strong!")
