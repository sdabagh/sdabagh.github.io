🟢 1. Today's letter — ر (ra)

Trace each ra. Start at the top. Curve down and to the left — like a little hook that dips below the line.

ر . . . . ر . . . . ر . . . . ر . . . . ر

Now try writing one by yourself:

Big ra! Make it huge — let the tail dip way down:

Remember: ra sits ON the line, then its tail drops BELOW the line. It's a swooper!

🟡 2. Find the ra

Circle every word that has the letter ر (ra) in it:

أَشرَب     عَصير     حَليب     رَيب     ماء     شاي

Hint: three of them have a ra. Can you find all three?

🟠 3. I can read these words

Read these three words out loud. Point to each one as you say it:

Now try a whole sentence:

**أنا أَشرَب ماء.**

ana ashrab maa — "I drink water."

Say it three times. Pat the table once for every word.

🟣 4. Match the drink to where it goes

Draw a line from each drink to the cup or glass it usually goes in.

There's no wrong answer — drinks go where YOUR family puts them!

🌟 6. Mini-challenge (optional stretch)

Have you ever tried رَيب (rayb)? It's a salty yogurt drink — kids in Lebanon, Syria, and Jordan drink it with lunch, especially on hot days. Some people call it ayran.

Circle your answer:

Have you tried رَيب? yes no I want to try it!

If you've had it, draw a tiny face showing how it tasted:
