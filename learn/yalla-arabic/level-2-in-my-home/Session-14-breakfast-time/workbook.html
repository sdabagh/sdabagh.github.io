🟢 1. Today's letter — ذ (dhal)

Trace each dhal. Start at the top curve. Pull down and around. Don't forget the dot on top!

ذ . . . . ذ . . . . ذ . . . . ذ . . . . ذ

Now try writing one by yourself:

Big dhal! Make it huge (don't forget the dot ✨):

*Tip: ذ sounds like the th in "this" — not like "thumb." Put your tongue between your teeth and buzz.*

🟡 2. Find the dhal

Circle every word that has the letter ذ (dhal) in it:

ذَوقي     خُبز     لَذيذ     زَيتون     هَذا     شَاي

Hint: three of them have a dhal. Look carefully for the dot on top!

🟠 3. I can read these words

🌟 6. Mini-challenge (optional stretch)

In Levantine Arabic, when you LOVE something you eat, you say:

**لَذيذ!**
*(la-zeez! — yummy! delicious!)*

See the ذ hiding in there? TWO of them!

Fill in the blank. What's your ذَوقي (your taste — what YOU like)?

**ذَوقي:**
_______________________ *(write a food you love — in English or Arabic!)*

Now say out loud: لَذيذ!
