🟢 1. Today's letter — ش (shin)

Trace each shin. Start with the bowl on the right. Add three little dots on top — like sprinkles!

ش . . . . ش . . . . ش . . . . ش . . . . ش

Now try writing one by yourself:

Big shin with big sprinkles! Make it huge:

*Shin says "sh" — like in shhh and shoe.*

🟡 2. Find the shin

Circle every word that has the letter ش (shin) in it:

شَعر     فَم     شُكراً     أَنف     شَمس

Hint: look for the three little dots on top!
