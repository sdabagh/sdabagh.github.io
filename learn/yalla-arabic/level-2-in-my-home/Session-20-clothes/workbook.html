🟢 1. Letter review — trace and find

Today we're reviewing letters you already know. Trace each one. Say its sound out loud as you write.

ب . . . . ب . . . . ب . . . . ب
ق . . . . ق . . . . ق . . . . ق
ك . . . . ك . . . . ك . . . . ك

Now write one of each by yourself:

ب ق ك

🟡 2. Find the letter

Circle every clothes word that has the letter ق (qaf) in it:

قَميص     بَنطَلون     كَنزة     صُبّاط     جاكيت

Now go back and underline every word with the letter ب (baa).

Hint: more than one word has a baa!

🟠 3. I can read these words

Read these three words out loud. Point to each picture as you say it:

Picture Arabic Say it
👕
قَميص
 qa-mees
👖
بَنطَلون
 ban-ta-lone
👟
صُبّاط
 sub-baat

After you read them, look down at what you're wearing right now. Are any of these on you today?

🟣 4. Match the clothes to the weather

Draw a line from each piece of clothing to the weather you'd wear it in.

Clothes Weather
جاكيت
 ─ → ☀️ Hot summer day
كَنزة
 ─ → ❄️ Cold and windy
قَميص
 ─ → 🍂 Cool fall morning

Some answers could go more than one way — that's okay! Talk about why with your grown-up.
