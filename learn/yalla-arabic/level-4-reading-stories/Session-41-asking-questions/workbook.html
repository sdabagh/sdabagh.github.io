🟢 1. Today's letter — ه (ha)

Trace each ha. Notice the little loop — like a tiny balloon on a string.

ه . . . . ه . . . . ه . . . . ه . . . . ه

Now try writing one by yourself:

The letter ha changes shape depending on where it is in the word. Trace all four:

ه    ـه    ـهـ    هـ
start middle end alone

🟡 2. Find the ha

Circle every word that has the letter ه (ha) in it:

هَل؟     ماذا؟     هُنا     مين؟     هَذا     كيف؟

Hint: ha shows up A LOT in question words and pointing words.

🟠 3. I can read these questions

Now ask a grown-up one of these questions about something in the room. Point at something and say شو؟ See what happens!
