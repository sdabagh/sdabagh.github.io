🟡 2. Find the nun

Circle every word that has the letter ن (nun) in it:

عِندي     كِتاب     حَيَوان     أَخ     أنا     بَيت

🌟 6. Mini-challenge (optional stretch)

Ask someone in your house: "إِلَك أَخ؟" (Do you have a brother?) — use إِلِك if you're asking a girl.

Write down what they said:

I asked... They said...
إِلَك / إِلِك _____________ ؟
عِندي / ما عِندي

Try asking about three different things: a brother, a sister, a pet. In Arabic, عِندي means "with me" — that's how Arabic says "I have." There's no separate word for "have." Cool, right?
