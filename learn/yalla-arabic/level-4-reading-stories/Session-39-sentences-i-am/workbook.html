🟢 1. Today's letter — م (mim)

Trace each mim. Start with the little round head, then pull the tail down.

م . . . . م . . . . م . . . . م . . . . م

Now try writing one by yourself:

Mim shows up at the start, middle, and end of words. Look how it changes shape:

**مَ** (start)     **ـمـ** (middle)     **ـم** (end)

🟡 2. Find the mim

Circle every word that has the letter م (mim) in it. It can hide anywhere — front, middle, or end.

ماما     سَعيد     مَع     بَيت     كَبير     سَلام

How many did you circle? Write the number here: _____

🟠 3. I can read these sentences

Read these out loud. Point to each word as you say it. These are full sentences — you are reading Arabic sentences now!

Feeling Arabic Say it
😊
أنا سَعيد
 ana sa'id
🏠
أنا في البَيت
 ana fi al-bayt
👩
أنا مَع ماما
 ana ma'a mama

If you're a girl, try:

أنا سَعيدة
(ana sa'ideh)
