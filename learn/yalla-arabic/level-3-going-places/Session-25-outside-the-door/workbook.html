🟢 1. Today's letter — ض (Dad)

Trace each ض. Start with the loop, then the tooth on top, then the dot way up high.

ض . . . . ض . . . . ض . . . . ض . . . . ض

Now try writing one by yourself:

Big ض! Make it huge — don't forget the dot:

Fun fact: Arabic is sometimes called "the language of ض" because this sound is so special.

🟡 2. Find the ض

Circle every word that has the letter ض in it:

ضَوء     شَمس     أَبيَض     قَمَر     أَرض

Hint: three of them have a ض. Look for the dot up high!

🟣 4. Sort it: Day or Night?

Draw a line from each word to where you'd see it — in the day sky or the night sky.

🌟 6. Mini-challenge (optional stretch)

The word ضَوء means light. Light comes from lots of places. Check the boxes for things that give light:

  • شَمس
    (sun)
  • قَمَر
    (moon)
  • نَجمة
    (star)
  • a lamp in your room 💡
  • a phone screen 📱
  • a rock 🪨

The moon is tricky! It doesn't make its own ضَوء — it bounces the sun's light back to us. Sneaky قَمَر.
