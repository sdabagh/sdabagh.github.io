🟢 1. Today's letter — ع ('ayn)

Trace each 'ayn. Start at the top of the little hook. Go around like a backwards "c," then curve down.

ع . . . . ع . . . . ع . . . . ع . . . . ع

Now try writing one by yourself:

Big 'ayn! Make it huge:

Tip: 'ayn is a "throat letter." Put your hand on your throat and feel it squeeze a little when you say it.

🟡 2. Find the 'ayn

Circle every word that has the letter ع ('ayn) in it:

عُصفور     قِطّة     حَيَوان     نَعرِف     كَلب     سَبعة

Hint: look carefully — 'ayn can hide in the middle of a word too!

🟠 3. I can read these words
