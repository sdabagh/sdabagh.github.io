🟢 1. Today's letter — ظ (Za)

Trace each ẓa. Start with the loop, then add the tall stick going up, then the dot on top.

ظ . . . . ظ . . . . ظ . . . . ظ . . . . ظ

Now try writing one by yourself:

Big ẓa! Make it huge — don't forget the dot:

Tip: ظ sounds heavy and deep, like you're saying it from the back of your mouth.

🟡 2. Find the ẓa

Circle every word that has the letter ظ in it:

ظَريف     أَحمَر     نَظيف     لَون     مَحفَظة

Hint: three of them have a ظ. ظَريف means "cute/nice," نَظيف means "clean," and مَحفَظة is a wallet.

🟠 3. I can read these colors
