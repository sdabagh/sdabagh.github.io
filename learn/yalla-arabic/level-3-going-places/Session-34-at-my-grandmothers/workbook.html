🟢 1. Letter review — write your favorites

Today is a review day — no new letter! Pick three letters you already know and write each one three times. Show off your best ones.

My letters:
Letter 1 Letter 2 Letter 3

Which one feels easiest now? Circle it. Which one is still tricky? Put a little star ⭐ next to it.

🟡 2. Find the letters — teta hunt

Look at the word تيتا (teta). It has the letter ت in it — twice!

Circle every word below that has a ت in it:

تيتا     حِكاية     بَيتي     كُك     اِشتَقتِلَّك     قَعَدنا

Hint: four of them have a ت somewhere. Look carefully — sometimes it's at the start, sometimes hiding in the middle.

🌟 6. Mini-challenge (optional stretch)

Build a tiny sentence about visiting teta. Fill in the blanks:

قَعَدنا عِند تيتا وَأَكَلنا _________ .

(We sat at teta's and we ate _________ .)

What did you eat? Write the Arabic word if you know it (كُك? أُكِل بَيتي?) or write it in English. Either way counts!
