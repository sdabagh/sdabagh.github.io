🟢 1. Today's letter — ف (fa)

Trace each fa. Start with the little circle on top, then pull the line down and across.

ف . . . . ف . . . . ف . . . . ف . . . . ف

Now try writing one by yourself:

Big fa! Make it huge — don't forget the dot on top:

Tip: ف has ONE dot above. Just one little dot. Easy.

🟡 2. Find the fa

Circle every word that has the letter ف (fa) in it:

فيروز     بَحر     غَيمة     فُل     جَبَل     فَراشة     رَمل

Hint: three of them have a fa. Look for the dot on top!

After you read them, tell a grown-up which one YOU like better — the sea or the mountain? In Lebanon, you can see both on the same day.

Write each word in the right column. Some could go in both — that's okay, you decide!
