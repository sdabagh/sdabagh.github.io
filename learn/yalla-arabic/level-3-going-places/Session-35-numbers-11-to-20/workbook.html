🟢 1. Today's numbers — trace and count

Today we're not learning a letter — we're learning numerals! Trace each number, then say it out loud.

١١ . . . . ١٢ . . . . ١٣ . . . . ١٤ . . . . ١٥

Now write each one yourself:

11 12 13 14 15

Big twenty! Make it huge:
٢٠

🟡 2. Find the number

Circle every Arabic number-word below that means more than ten:

إِحدَعَش     خَمسة     عِشرين     اِثنَعَش     ثَلاثة     خَمسطَعَش

Hint: four of them are bigger than ten!

🟠 3. I can read these numbers

Read these three words out loud. Point to each one as you say it:

Number Arabic Say it

12

20
عِشرين
 'ish-rin

After you read them, ask a grown-up: "Did I say it right?" Then count from 11 to 15 out loud — go!
