Day 23 · 15 minutes

Beautiful.ai

Build a presentation from an outline in under ten minutes.

~15 min read + 10 min hands-on

1 Why this tool matters

Beautiful.ai is a presentation tool whose AI layout engine keeps every slide looking polished as you add content. Its newer AI features (DesignerBot) will build an entire deck from a single prompt. For anyone who spends hours fighting with PowerPoint alignment, this is the escape hatch.

2 Walkthrough

  1. Go to beautiful.ai and start a trial.
  2. Click Generate with DesignerBot. Describe the deck: A 10-slide deck for a town hall on new company benefits. Cover: what's changing, who is affected, the timeline, the FAQ, and next steps. Confident, clear tone.
  3. Wait 45 seconds. Review the generated deck.
  4. Edit a slide by typing. Notice that the layout auto-adjusts as the content grows.
  5. Export to PowerPoint for the meeting — your audience will never know.

3 Your turn

Today’s exercise

Build one real deck you need for a meeting this week. Use DesignerBot to draft, then customize. Time yourself. The benchmark is <30 minutes, end to end.

4 Pro tip

Worth keeping

Beautiful.ai is at its best for 10-15 slide decks. For a 50-slide board report, it fights you. Use it for the decks where style matters more than density.
