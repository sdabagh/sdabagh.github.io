Day 25 · 15 minutes

Zapier AI

Automate a three-step workflow in five minutes.

~15 min read + 10 min hands-on

1 Why this tool matters

Zapier has been the universal glue between work apps for a decade. Its AI layer (Copilot) lets you describe a workflow in plain English, and it builds the “Zap” automation for you. If you have been thinking “someone should automate this,” this is where you become that someone.

2 Walkthrough

  1. Go to zapier.com. Sign up (free).
  2. Click Create Zap → Copilot.
  3. Describe a real need: When a new row is added to my Google Sheet of event RSVPs, send me a Slack message and email the attendee a confirmation.
  4. Copilot builds the Zap. Connect the accounts it asks for (Google, Slack, email).
  5. Test it with one row. Watch the chain fire.

3 Your turn

Today’s exercise

Automate one thing you do manually every week. Literally anything: RSS to email, form to spreadsheet, calendar to task list. Turn it on. You just bought back that recurring time.

4 Pro tip

Worth keeping

Zapier's AI Actions let you call ChatGPT/Claude inside a Zap, so an incoming email can be summarized and routed based on its content. Once you see that possibility, half your inbox can be automated.
