Day 24 · 15 minutes

Fathom

Never take notes in a Zoom meeting again.

~15 min read + 10 min hands-on

1 Why this tool matters

Fathom joins your Zoom, Google Meet, or Teams meeting as a bot, records it, transcribes it, and — the clever part — produces a summary with action items and timestamps while you are still saying goodbye. Free forever for the basic tier. This is the tool that most directly returns hours to your week.

2 Walkthrough

  1. Go to fathom.video and connect it to your calendar and Zoom/Meet/Teams.
  2. Schedule (or wait for) your next meeting. Fathom joins automatically.
  3. After the meeting, check your email: Fathom has sent the summary, action items, and highlights.
  4. Click a highlight to jump to that moment in the recording.
  5. Share the summary with the attendees as a follow-up note. You have replaced 20 minutes of post-meeting writing.

3 Your turn

Today’s exercise

Let Fathom sit in on your next three meetings this week. At the end of each, review the auto-summary before writing anything. Decide: do you still need human notes, or does Fathom's output cover it?

4 Pro tip

Worth keeping

Always tell the meeting it is being recorded. Some jurisdictions (and all of good manners) require it. Fathom has a setting to auto-announce on join.
