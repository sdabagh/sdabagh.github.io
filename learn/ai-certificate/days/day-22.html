Day 22 · 15 minutes

Jasper

Write marketing copy that sounds like a professional wrote it.

~15 min read + 10 min hands-on

1 Why this tool matters

Jasper is an AI writing platform built for marketers and content teams. Its edge is templates (email sequences, landing pages, social posts) plus brand-voice training — you give it samples of your writing, and it reproduces your tone across assets. For anyone who owns external comms, this is a force-multiplier.

2 Walkthrough

  1. Go to jasper.ai. Start the free trial.
  2. Create a Brand Voice: paste three samples of your existing writing (a blog post, a newsletter, a social caption). Jasper learns the voice.
  3. Pick the AIDA email template. Describe the product: A free Saturday workshop on retirement planning basics for busy parents.
  4. Jasper generates four variants. Edit the best one.
  5. Run the same product through the Landing Page Hero template. Notice the voice consistency.

3 Your turn

Today’s exercise

Train one brand voice on three pieces of your own writing. Then generate a marketing email in that voice for a real thing you are promoting. Save the brand voice — it is now reusable.

4 Pro tip

Worth keeping

Jasper shines for volume. For a one-off email, ChatGPT is fine. The moment you need 30 emails in the same voice, Jasper pays for itself. Don't over-adopt; match the tool to the volume.
