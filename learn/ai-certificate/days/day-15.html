Day 15 · 15 minutes

Canva Magic Studio

Design a flyer or one-pager in under five minutes.

~15 min read + 10 min hands-on

1 Why this tool matters

Canva quietly added AI to every corner of its design tool: generate text, generate images, write copy, remove backgrounds, translate, resize to any format. If your job involves making flyers, social graphics, or one-pagers, Canva Magic Studio is the productivity unlock.

2 Walkthrough

  1. Open canva.com. Click Create a design → Flyer.
  2. In the blank canvas, click Magic Design and describe what you need: A flyer announcing a weekend workshop on financial planning for families, warm colors, approachable.
  3. Canva generates several templates. Pick one.
  4. Use Magic Write to generate the body copy inline.
  5. Use Magic Eraser to clean up the stock photo. Use Magic Resize to make an Instagram version.

3 Your turn

Today’s exercise

Design one thing you actually need this week — a flyer, a social graphic, a poster for your fridge. Use at least three Magic Studio features. Export as PDF.

4 Pro tip

Worth keeping

Canva's AI is weakest at generating photo-real people. Lean on its illustrations and icons (which it does beautifully) and save the human photography for stock sources.
