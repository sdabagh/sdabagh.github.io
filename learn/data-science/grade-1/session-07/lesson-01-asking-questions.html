Lesson 1: Asking Questions About Data

About 30 minutes — Discussion-based lesson

What You Will Learn

This lesson covers:

  • Asking questions that data can answer

Your pictograph answers questions! Learn to ask: 'Which has the most? The fewest? How many more?'

This section covers the key ideas of this lesson. Discuss with your group or family and explore the concepts together through hands-on activities and conversation.

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the main idea of this lesson?

Answer: Your pictograph answers questions! Learn to ask: 'Which has the most? The fewest? How many more?'

2. Can you explain this concept to a family member in your own words?

Answer: Try explaining what you learned. If you can teach it, you understand it!

Key Takeaways

  • Your pictograph answers questions! Learn to ask: 'Which has the most? The fewest? How many more?'
  • Practice this skill at home with everyday objects
