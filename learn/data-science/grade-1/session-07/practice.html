Session 7: Practice Activities

Time to Practice!

6 activities to reinforce what you learned. Try them all!

Difficulty levels: Easy Medium Challenge

1 5 Questions About Your Pictograph Easy Activity

Write 5 questions that can be answered by looking at your pictograph. Then answer each one.

2 Find the Finding Easy Discussion

Look at a pictograph and write 3 'We found out that...' sentences. Share them with someone.

3 How Many More? Medium Activity

Look at your data. For each pair of groups, figure out how many MORE one has than the other. Write it down.

4 Teach a Family Member Medium Group Activity

Present your pictograph to someone at home. Explain: what you asked, how many people you asked, and what you found out.

5 Data Reporter Medium Creative

Pretend you are a TV reporter. Write a 'news report' about your data findings. Read it aloud dramatically!

6 Two Findings, One Graph Challenge Critical Thinking

Look at your pictograph and find TWO different things the data tells you. Sometimes the same data answers more than one question!
