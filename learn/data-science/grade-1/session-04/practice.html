Session 4: Practice Activities

Time to Practice!

6 activities to reinforce what you learned. Try them all!

Difficulty levels: Easy Medium Challenge

1 Snack Comparison Easy Activity

Count two types of snacks (crackers vs. grapes). Which has more? Use the words 'more than' and 'fewer than.'

2 Shoe Lineup Easy Activity

Gather shoes from your family. Line them up by family member. Who has the most shoes? Who has the fewest?

3 Coin Compare Medium Activity

Sort coins (or buttons) by type. Count each. Write comparison sentences: '___ has more than ___.'

4 Playground Comparison Medium Discussion

At a park, count kids on swings vs. slide vs. climbing. Compare all three using more/less/equal.

5 Family Favorites Medium Group Activity

Ask family members their favorite season. Count each. Write 3 comparison sentences about the results.

6 Three-Way Compare Challenge Critical Thinking

Sort 30 objects by color (3+ colors). Count each. Rank all groups from most to fewest. Write a full comparison paragraph.
