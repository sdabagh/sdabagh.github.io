Session 7: Privacy and Consent

Your data is valuable. Learn about digital privacy, informed consent, and the Islamic principle of Amana applied to information.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this session, you will be able to:

  • Explain what personal data is and why it is valuable
  • Understand informed consent and why it matters
  • Evaluate the privacy practices of apps and websites
  • Apply the concept of Amana to protecting others' information

Why This Matters

Every app, website, and service collects your data. Understanding privacy and consent gives you the power to protect yourself and others.
