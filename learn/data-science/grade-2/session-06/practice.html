Session 6: Practice Activities

Time to Practice!

6 activities to reinforce what you learned. Try them all!

Difficulty levels: Easy Medium Challenge

1 Boys vs. Girls Easy Activity

Ask boys and girls the same question (favorite sport). Build two bar graphs side by side. Compare!

2 Kids vs. Adults Easy Activity

Ask kids and adults their favorite season. Build two graphs. Where do they agree? Differ?

3 Two Classes Medium Group Activity

Survey two different classes or groups about the same thing. Build comparison graphs.

4 Same or Different? Medium Discussion

Write 3 sentences: 1 about what is the same, 1 about what is different, 1 about what surprised you.

5 Historical Comparison Medium Creative

Ask your parents what was popular when THEY were kids. Compare their answers to yours with two graphs!

6 Fair Comparison? Challenge Critical Thinking

If Group A has 30 people and Group B has 5, is comparing the totals fair? Why or why not? What could you do instead?
