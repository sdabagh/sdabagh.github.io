Session 7: What Does the Data Say?

Learn to look at your graphs and say what you found. Practice 'The data shows that...'

4 Lessons
~2 hours total
6 Practice Activities
Session Quiz

Your Progress

Lessons 1-4
Practice
Session Quiz
Review

Learning Objectives

By the end of this session, you will be able to:

  • Understanding that data tells a story
  • Using 'The data shows that...' sentence frame
  • Distinguishing between data facts and personal opinions
  • Presenting data findings clearly to others
