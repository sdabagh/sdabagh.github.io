Lesson Summaries

Lesson 1: Data Tells a Story

Every graph tells a story. Learn to see the story hidden in the bars, heights, and patterns.

Lesson 2: The Data Shows...

Practice the most important sentence in data science: 'The data shows that...' Use it for everything!

Lesson 3: Fact vs. Opinion

The data shows facts. 'Pizza is the best' is YOUR opinion. Learn to tell the difference.

Lesson 4: Show and Tell

Present a graph to your family using the sentence frame: 'I asked ___, the data shows ___, I learned ___.''
