Session 2: Practice Activities

Time to Practice!

6 activities to reinforce what you learned. Try them all!

Difficulty levels: Easy Medium Challenge

1 Measure Your Hand Easy Activity

Measure your hand span (thumb to pinky, spread wide) in inches. Measure a parent's too. Compare!

2 Five Objects Easy Activity

Measure 5 objects on your desk. Record each in a table: Object | Length. Circle the longest.

3 Family Foot Lengths Medium Activity

Measure every family member's foot. Record in a table. Who has the longest foot? The shortest?

4 Estimate Then Measure Medium Activity

Pick 5 objects. GUESS the length first, then measure. How close were your guesses? Write both numbers.

5 Measurement Scavenger Hunt Medium Activity

Find something shorter than 3 inches, something between 6-8 inches, and something longer than 12 inches. Measure to prove it!

6 Unit Confusion Challenge Challenge Critical Thinking

Your friend says a table is '30 long.' 30 WHAT? Inches? Feet? Centimeters? Why does the unit change everything?
