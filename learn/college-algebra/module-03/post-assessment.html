This post-assessment measures what you've learned about polynomial and rational functions after completing the module. Comparing your pre- and post-assessment results helps demonstrate your learning progress!

Question 1

Question 2

Question 3

Question 4

If p(x) = -3x4 + 6x2 - x + 9, which statement best describes the end behavior as x approaches positive infinity?

Question 5
