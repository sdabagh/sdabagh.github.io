Academic Writing with AI

Use Claude and other AI tools as a 24/7 writing tutor — ethically, effectively, and with your own voice intact.

8 Modules ~25 min each Free Ethics-First College Level

⚠️ Before We Start: The Honest Truth About This Course

This course teaches you to USE AI as a tool — not to have AI write for you. There’s a critical difference. Your professor is grading YOUR thinking, not Claude’s. We’ll help you find the line between using AI as a tutor and using it as a ghostwriter. That line matters for your education, your integrity, and frankly, your career.
