Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, your child will be able to:

  • Solve logic puzzles using reasoning and careful thinking
  • Explain how strategy games teach us to think ahead and plan
  • Connect coding thinking skills to school subjects and everyday life
  • Describe what they learned across all 8 modules of this course
  • Identify resources and activities to keep learning and growing

Why This Matters

Thinking like a coder is not just about computers. The skills your child has learned -- breaking problems into steps, spotting patterns, making decisions, finding and fixing mistakes, and planning before building -- are skills that help in every part of life.

This module shows your child that they are now a stronger thinker than when they started. And it gives them a path to keep growing long after this course is done.

Tips for This Module

  • Have fun! This module is about enjoying the thinking skills you have built. There is no pressure.
  • Try the puzzles together. Logic puzzles are more fun when you solve them as a team.
  • Play the games for real. Actually play tic-tac-toe and Nim together -- do not just read about them.
  • Celebrate the journey. Look back at Module 1 and see how far your child has come.
  • Print the certificate. It is a real achievement and worth putting on the wall!
