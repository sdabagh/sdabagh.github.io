Phase 5 of 6

SAP FI/CO

Deep dive into enterprise financial systems. The rare skill that opens Fortune 500 doors — and this course doesn’t skip or simplify it.

3 Modules ~70 Minutes Total openSAP.com Enterprise Level

🏢 Why SAP FI/CO is in this course

77% of global transaction revenue passes through SAP. That means virtually every large company — hospital systems, banks, manufacturers, retailers, governments — runs SAP. SAP FI/CO consultants earn $85,000–$140,000+ USD. The combination of data analytics skills AND SAP knowledge is rare and highly valued.

This is not a surface-level introduction. These three modules go deep. You will understand the organizational structure, the key sub-modules, real transaction codes, the FI-CO integration, and basic configuration. That is the level that gets you hired.

Before starting: Create a free account at opensap.com — SAP’s official free learning platform.

✓ What you will know by the end of Phase 5

  • What SAP is, what S/4HANA is, and why companies pay so much for SAP professionals
  • SAP FI: General Ledger, Accounts Payable, Accounts Receivable, Asset Accounting
  • Key FI transaction codes: FB50, FB60, FB70, F110, FBL1N, FBL5N, FS10N
  • SAP CO: Cost Center Accounting, Profit Center Accounting, Internal Orders
  • Key CO transaction codes: KS01, KSB1, KE51, KO01, KO88
  • The FI-CO integration: how one posting creates two records
  • SAP configuration basics via SPRO and the IMG
  • SAP vocabulary you can use in job interviews with confidence
