💼 Why a portfolio matters more than a certificate

Anyone can list “SQL” on a resume. Not everyone can show a live dashboard and a cleaned dataset. Your portfolio is proof. One solid project — a question you asked, data you cleaned, a visualization you built — sets you apart from most applicants who only have coursework to show.

✓ What you will have when you finish Phase 4

  • A GitHub account and your first public repository
  • A cleaned dataset with documented data quality notes
  • One specific question answered with data
  • One published visualization (Tableau Public or Power BI)
  • A README that tells the story of your project
  • A portfolio URL you can share with employers
