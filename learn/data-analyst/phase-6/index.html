🧠 A note for neurodivergent job seekers

Interviews are designed by neurotypical people for neurotypical people. That is the truth. But there are strategies that work, accommodations you are entitled to request, and ways to use your ND traits as genuine strengths. Phase 6 covers all of it — not as an afterthought, but as a central part of your job strategy.

✓ What you will have when you finish Phase 6

  • Written answers to the most common data analyst interview questions
  • Written answers to the most common SAP FI/CO interview questions
  • A practiced “Tell me about yourself” answer (60–90 seconds)
  • A 30-60-90 day plan for your first data role
  • A personal system for your first weeks on the job
  • Confidence based on preparation, not performance
