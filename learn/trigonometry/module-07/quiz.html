Module 7 Quiz: Applications of Trigonometry

10 Questions

1

State the Law of Sines.

a/sinA = b/sinB = c/sinC
2

State the Law of Cosines for side c.

c² = a² + b² − 2ab cosC
3

When does SSA give two solutions?

When A is acute and h < a < b (where h = b sinA).
4

a=8, b=6, C=90°. Find c.

c=√(64+36)=10 (Pythagorean theorem, cos90=0).
5

Area formula with two sides and included angle?

A = (1/2)ab sinC
6

Heron's formula: s = (a+b+c)/2. Area = ?

√[s(s−a)(s−b)(s−c)]
7

|⟨−3, 4⟩| = ?

5
8

Component form of a vector with magnitude 6 and direction 30°?

⟨6cos30, 6sin30⟩ = ⟨3√3, 3⟩
9

What is a unit vector?

A vector with magnitude 1: v/|v|.
10

⟨2,3⟩ + ⟨5,−1⟩ = ?

⟨7, 2⟩
