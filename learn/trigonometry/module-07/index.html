Module 7: Applications of Trigonometry

Apply the Law of Sines, Law of Cosines, triangle area formulas, and get introduced to vectors.

4 Lessons
~4 hours
10 Practice Problems
Module Quiz

Learning Objectives

  • Apply the Law of Sines including the ambiguous case (SSA)
  • Apply the Law of Cosines to solve oblique triangles
  • Calculate triangle area using trig and Heron's formula
  • Define vectors, find magnitude and direction, and perform basic operations
