Module 4: Graphs of Trigonometric Functions

Graph sine, cosine, tangent, cotangent, secant, and cosecant with transformations.

4 Lessons
~4 hours
10 Practice Problems
Module Quiz

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Graph y = A sin(Bx − C) + D and y = A cos(Bx − C) + D identifying amplitude, period, phase shift, and vertical shift
  • Graph tangent and cotangent functions with their asymptotes
  • Graph secant and cosecant using their reciprocal relationships
  • Apply transformations to create accurate sketches of all six trig functions
