Module 4 Quiz: Graphs of Trig Functions

Test Your Knowledge

10 questions on graphing all six trig functions.

1

Amplitude and period of y = 5 cos(πx)?

Amp = 5, Period = 2π/π = 2.
2

Phase shift of y = sin(2x − π/3)?

C/B = (π/3)/2 = π/6 right.
3

Range of y = −3 sin(x) + 5?

[2, 8].
4

Period of y = tan(4x)?

π/4.
5

Where are asymptotes of y = sec(x)?

At x = π/2 + nπ (where cos = 0).
6

y = cos x starts at what value when x = 0?

1 (its maximum).
7

What is the midline of y = 2 sin(x) + 4?

y = 4.
8

Range of y = csc(x)?

(−∞, −1] ∪ [1, ∞).
9

Period of y = cot(x/2)?

π/(1/2) = 2π.
10

Is y = −sin(x) a reflection across which axis?

Reflection across the x-axis.
