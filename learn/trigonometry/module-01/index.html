Module 1: Angles and Their Measure

In this module you will learn how angles are measured in both degrees and radians, and apply these measurements to arc length, sector area, and speed problems.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Classify angles by type (acute, right, obtuse, straight, reflex) and by standard position
  • Convert between degree and radian measures fluently
  • Calculate arc length and area of a sector using radian measure
  • Solve problems involving angular and linear speed
  • Apply angle measurement to real-world contexts such as wheels, clocks, and navigation
