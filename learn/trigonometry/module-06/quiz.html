Module 6 Quiz: Trig Equations

Test Your Knowledge

10 questions on solving trig equations and inverse functions.

1

Solve sinθ=√2/2 on [0,2π).

π/4, 3π/4
2

arctan(0) = ?

0
3

Solve 2cosθ+1=0 on [0,2π).

2π/3, 4π/3
4

General solution of tanθ=√3?

θ=π/3+nπ
5

arccos(1) = ?

0
6

Solve sin(2x)=0 on [0,2π). How many solutions?

4: x=0, π/2, π, 3π/2.
7

Range of arcsin?

[−π/2, π/2]
8

Solve cos²θ=1 on [0,2π).

cosθ=±1: 0, π
9

Find sin(arccos(3/5)).

4/5
10

arcsin(sin(2π/3)) = ?

sin(2π/3)=√3/2. arcsin(√3/2)=π/3 (not 2π/3).
