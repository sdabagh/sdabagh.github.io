Module 3 Quiz: Trig Functions of Any Angle

Test Your Knowledge

10 questions. Aim for 7+ correct!

1

The point (5, −12) is on the terminal side. Find sin θ.

r = 13. sin = −12/13.
2

Evaluate cos(240°).

QIII, ref 60°. cos 60° = 1/2. Negative in QIII: −1/2.
3

Simplify: csc²θ − cot²θ

1 (Pythagorean identity).
4

If cos θ = 5/13 and θ in QIV, find tan θ.

sin = −12/13 (QIV). tan = −12/5. −12/5.
5

Is tangent even or odd?

Odd: tan(−θ) = −tan θ.
6

Simplify: sin(−45°)

−sin 45° = −√2/2.
7

Express sec 20° as a cofunction.

sec 20° = csc 70°.
8

Simplify: tan θ cot θ

1 (reciprocal identity).
9

Find csc(7π/6).

sin(7π/6) = −1/2 (QIII, ref π/6). csc = −2.
10

Simplify: (sin²θ)(sec²θ)

= sin²θ/cos²θ = tan²θ.
