Module 5 Quiz: Trigonometric Identities

Test Your Knowledge

10 questions on trig identities and formulas.

1

cos(A+B) = ?

cosAcosB − sinAsinB
2

sin2θ = ?

2sinθcosθ
3

Find cos15° exactly.

cos(45−30) = (√6+√2)/4
4

cos2θ has how many equivalent forms?

3: cos²−sin², 2cos²−1, 1−2sin²
5

sin²x = ? (power-reducing)

(1 − cos2x)/2
6

Verify: secθ − cosθ = sinθtanθ

LHS = 1/cos − cos = (1−cos²)/cos = sin²/cos = sin·(sin/cos) = sin·tan.
7

tan(A−B) formula?

(tanA − tanB)/(1 + tanAtanB)
8

Write sin3x cos2x as a sum.

(1/2)[sin5x + sinx]
9

cos(θ/2) = ? (half-angle)

±√[(1+cosθ)/2]
10

cosA+cosB as a product?

2cos((A+B)/2)cos((A−B)/2)
