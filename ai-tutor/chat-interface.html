How to Use the AI Tutor

Getting Started

Ask questions in your own words about any statistics concept. The AI tutor adapts its responses based on your needs.

Support Levels

  • Level 1 (Hints): Minimal guidance to encourage independent thinking
  • Level 2 (Guided): Step-by-step questions to walk through problems
  • Level 3 (Examples): Worked examples and detailed explanations

The tutor automatically adjusts the support level based on your progress.

Quick Actions

Use the buttons above the input box for common requests:

  • Hint: Get a small clue to point you in the right direction
  • Explain Step-by-Step: Break down the solution process
  • Show Example: See a worked example of a similar problem

Best Practices

  • Be specific about what you're struggling with
  • Try the problem yourself before asking for help
  • Ask follow-up questions if you need clarification
  • Let the tutor know if an explanation doesn't make sense

Research Participation

Your interactions help us understand how AI can best support statistics learning. All data is anonymized and used only for educational research.

Privacy & Data Collection

What Data We Collect

  • Chat messages (questions and responses)
  • Timestamps and session duration
  • Module and lesson context
  • Scaffolding level adjustments
  • Anonymous user ID (no personal information)

How We Use This Data

All data is used exclusively for educational research on AI-powered cognitive scaffolding. Your data helps us understand:

  • How students interact with AI tutoring systems
  • Which types of support are most effective
  • How to improve adaptive learning algorithms

Your Rights

  • Participation is voluntary
  • You may stop using the tutor at any time
  • Data is anonymized and cannot be traced back to you
  • Results will be published in aggregate form only
