4 Think Deeper — Fair Comparisons

Class A has 28 students total. Class B has 34 students total. This means raw numbers might not be totally fair to compare.

Complete the fractions for cats:

Class A cats as a fraction: 7 out of 28 = _____ out of every 4 students
Class B cats as a fraction: 11 out of 34 ≈ _____ out of every 4 students (estimate)

After looking at these fractions, does Class B still seem more into cats than Class A? Explain.
