PHASE 5: WRITE
Tell your data story in at least 3 sentences
47 – 57 min
Sentence 1 — My question and sample size:
"My question was: _____. I surveyed _____ people."
Sentence 2 — Most common answer (with numbers):
"The most common answer was _____, chosen by _____ out of _____ people."
Sentence 3 — Something surprising or unexpected:
"I was surprised that _____ because I expected _____."
Sentence 4 — What the data means (interpretation):
"My data shows that most people _____, which means _____."
Sentence 5 — A new question your data makes you wonder:
"If I could survey more people, I would want to find out _____."
