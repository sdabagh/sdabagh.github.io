Sentence Frames

How to Answer Graph Questions

Use these sentence frames to write or say your answers:

"The most popular ___ was ___ with ___ votes."
"The least popular ___ was ___ with ___ votes."
"___ got ___ more votes than ___."
"___ and ___ got the same number of votes."

These frames work for ANY bar graph — use them every time you answer a graph question!

How Many More?

Finding "How Many More"

To find how many MORE one bar has than another:

  • 1️⃣ Find the number for the bigger bar
  • 2️⃣ Find the number for the smaller bar
  • 3️⃣ Count up from the smaller to the bigger
    OR subtract: bigger − smaller = difference

Example: Strawberry = 9, Banana = 4
9 − 4 = 5 more votes
