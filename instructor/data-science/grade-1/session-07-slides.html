Lesson 1

Data Statements 📢

A data statement is a sentence that tells what a graph shows.

  • ✅ It uses what you can SEE in the graph
  • ✅ It can use numbers OR comparison words
  • ✅ It is a fact — anyone can check it!

"Apple has the most." ✅    "Apple has 6 stickers." ✅
Both are great data statements! 🌟

5 / 15
Sentence Frames

Use These Frames! 📋

Fill in the blank to make a data statement:

  • "This graph is about ___."
  • "The most ___ is ___."
  • "The least ___ is ___."
  • "___ has more than ___."
  • "We found out that ___."
  • "I notice that ___."
