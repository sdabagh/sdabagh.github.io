Lesson 1

We Use Our Senses 🌟

When we observe, we use our senses on purpose and carefully.

👀 See

  • Color
  • Shape
  • Size

🖐️ Touch

  • Smooth or bumpy?
  • Soft or hard?
  • Big or small?

Observing is slow and careful. We pay attention!

6 / 15
Lesson 2

Same and Different 🔄

Two things can be the same in one way and different in another way!

🔵 Same color!

A blue button and a blue block are BOTH blue.

🔷 Different shape!

The button is round. The block is square. They are different shapes.

The same objects can be the same in some ways and different in others! 🌟

Activity Time!

Partner Mystery Bag! 🤝

Work with your partner. Here is what you do:

  • 🖐️ Partner A — reach in. Say 2 things you feel.
  • ✏️ Partner B — draw what you imagine!
  • 👀 Both — look inside. What more do you see?
  • 🔄 Swap! Partner B reaches in next.

It is okay to just LOOK instead of touching. Both ways count! 👍

Brain Break — Slow Animal Walk!

Debrief

What Did You Find? 🔍

  • What did you feel first?
  • Were you surprised when you looked?
  • What could you feel but NOT see?
  • What could you see but NOT feel?

Using senses together tells us MORE than using just one! 🌟

12 / 15
Think About It

Observe vs. Guess 🤔

✅ Observe

  • It IS round
  • It IS blue
  • It IS smooth

You can check — it is true!

❓ Guess

  • It is pretty
  • It is my favorite
  • It is the best

Different people might say different things!
