Lesson 1

Three Comparing Words 🔤

MORE ↑
Has a higher number than the other
FEWER ↓
Has a lower number than the other
SAME =
Has the exact same number as the other

We use these words to compare — to look at two things and see how they are different!

Lesson 2

Most and Least 🏆

Most = the column with the highest number of all.
Least = the column with the lowest number of all.

🏆 MOST

The tallest column.
The biggest number.

🔻 LEAST

The shortest column.
The smallest number.

You can find most and least just by looking — no counting needed! 👀
