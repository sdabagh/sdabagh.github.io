Lesson 1

What Is a Survey? 📋

A survey is when you:

  • ❓ Ask the same question to many people
  • ✋ Each person gives their one answer — that is their vote
  • ✅ You record every answer
  • 🔢 You count how many said each thing

Today our answers are only YES or NO — just two choices! 🟢 🔴

Lesson 2

How We Record Answers ✅

For each YES answer, the teacher draws a check mark ✓ in the YES column.
For each NO answer, a check mark in the NO column.

✅ YES

✓ ✓ ✓ ✓ ✓

5 people said yes

❌ NO

✓ ✓ ✓

3 people said no

Each check mark = one person's answer. Count the checks to find the total!
