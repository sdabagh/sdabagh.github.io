Your Mystery Bag 🎒

DO NOT OPEN YET! 🛑

Inside your bag are mixed-up objects.

Your job: figure out what is inside

and make a graph that shows what you found!

When your teacher says GO — open your bag! 🟢

Step 1: SORT 📦

Spread out ALL your objects.

Look at them carefully. What do you notice?

Choose ONE sort rule:

Color?    Shape?    Size?    Texture?

Put objects into groups.

Each object belongs in ONE group.

Draw each group on your worksheet!

Step 2: COUNT 🔢

Count how many are in each group.

Touch each one as you count: 1, 2, 3…

Write the NUMBER

5

Write it big in the box!

Draw DOTS

● ● ●
● ●

One dot = one object

Do this for EVERY group in your bag.

Draw your pictograph on the worksheet.

One star (or dot or smiley!) = one object.

Label each column with the group name.

Step 4: SHARE 🎤

Use a sentence frame to say what you found:

"We found out that ."
"The most is ."
"I notice that ."

Fill in ONE frame on your worksheet.

Then you will share it with the class!
