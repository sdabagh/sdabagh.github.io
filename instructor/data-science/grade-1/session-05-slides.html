Lesson 1

What Is a Pictograph?

A pictograph is a graph where each picture stands for one thing.

⭐ One sticker = one person

  • Every person gets exactly ONE sticker
  • You place it in the column that matches YOUR answer
  • When everyone is done, we can READ the graph!
5 / 15
Lesson 1

What Is a Column?

A column is a line of stickers going UP — like a tower!

Dog
5 people
Cat
3 people
Bird
2 people

The tallest column has the most people! You can see it just by looking!

6 / 15
Lesson 2

One Sticker = One Person ⭐

Your sticker means:

  • → YOU answered this question
  • → YOUR answer is in that column
  • → YOUR sticker is part of our data

Remember:

  • → ONE sticker only
  • → In YOUR column
  • → In the next empty row

8 / 15
Watch First!

Teacher Goes First

Watch your teacher place their sticker on the wall chart.

  • 1⃣ Walk to the chart
  • 2⃣ Find your column (your favorite animal)
  • 3⃣ Find the lowest empty row
  • 4⃣ Place your sticker there carefully
  • 5⃣ Walk back to your seat

Take your time! There is no rush.

9 / 15
Your Turn!

Place Your Sticker! ⭐

Your teacher will call your group. When it's your turn:

  • Walk to the wall chart
  • Find YOUR column
  • ⬆ Place your sticker in the next empty spot
  • Walk back quietly

While you wait — watch the chart grow! Notice which column is getting taller.

10 / 15
Brain Break — Be Your Animal!

Stand up and move like your favorite animal for 30 seconds!

Wag · Stretch · Flap · Hop · Crawl slowly

Sit back down when you hear the clap!

11 / 15
Look at Our Graph!

What Do We See?

Stand back and look at our whole class pictograph.

  • Which column is the tallest?
  • Which column is the shortest?
  • Can you count the stickers in each column?
  • Does the tallest column surprise you?

We built this graph together — every sticker is one of us!

12 / 15
Worksheet Time

Draw Your Mini Graph

On your worksheet, draw a mini version of our class pictograph:

  • Draw or place dots for each person in each column
  • Label each column with the animal name
  • Write the number of stickers in each column

Look at the wall chart to copy — that is the right thing to do!

Words We Learned

Our New Words

Pictograph
A graph where each picture or sticker stands for one person
Symbol ⭐
The sticker or picture that stands for one person
Column
A line of stickers going up
More / Fewer ↕
More = higher column · Fewer = shorter column
