🔍 Part 5 — Error Detective

Read each story. Circle the mistake and explain what the data collector should have done differently.

🔎 Scenario 1
Mia is collecting data about favorite colors. For the first 5 people she writes the color names (like "blue" or "red"). For the last 5 people, she uses tally marks instead. When she tries to count her results, everything is mixed up.
What is the error?
What should Mia have done?
🔎 Scenario 2
Sam is asking classmates "What is your favorite sport?" But every time he asks, he adds "…most people say soccer, just so you know!" before they answer.
What is the error?
What should Sam have done?
🔎 Scenario 3
Dev needs to survey 10 people. He surveys 9 people, then realizes he forgot to count his best friend's answer — so he goes back and adds it. But he had already asked his best friend at the beginning!
What is the error?
What should Dev have done?
