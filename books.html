You're Smarter Than You Think

College Algebra for STEM Majors

A complete, neurodivergent-friendly College Algebra textbook — free for every student at Santa Monica College, West Los Angeles College, and anywhere else in the world.

Download Free PDF Adopt for Your Course

Designed Differently — On Purpose

Traditional algebra textbooks assume you learn by reading definitions and memorizing procedures. This book assumes you're human. Every section starts with a story — a real-life moment that is the math concept, before we ever name it.

Story First

Every section begins with a real-world scenario. Math concepts are introduced through context, not definitions.

Consistent Rhythm

Every section follows the same 6-step structure. Your brain knows what's coming, so it can focus on the math.

Desmos Throughout

Every chapter has interactive Desmos explorations — see the math visually before calculating it.

Check Your Voice

Every section includes a metacognitive moment to notice — and talk back to — that inner critic.

Complete Answer Keys

Every practice problem has a worked solution. No hunting. No guessing.

Neurodivergent-Friendly

Designed for students who think differently — ADHD, dyslexia, anxiety, first-generation learners.

Statistics in the Eye of a Nurse

A Clinical Approach to Data-Informed Practice

An introductory statistics textbook designed specifically for nursing and health sciences students at Joyce University of Nursing and Health Sciences. Every example, every dataset, every problem connects statistics to clinical practice.

Download Free PDF Adopt for Your Course

Why This Book?

Most statistics textbooks use generic examples that feel disconnected from nursing practice. This book bridges that gap — every concept is taught through clinical scenarios that nurses encounter in their daily work.

Clinical Context

Every example uses real-world healthcare data — patient outcomes, medication dosages, vital signs, and clinical trials.

Evidence-Based Practice

Learn to read, interpret, and apply research findings to improve patient care.

Aligned with Sullivan 6e

Designed as a companion to Sullivan's Statistics: Informed Decisions Using Data, 6th Edition.

Complete Coverage

From descriptive statistics to hypothesis testing — everything you need for MAT 300.

Course: MAT 300 · Introduction to Statistics

Institution: Joyce University of Nursing and Health Sciences
